About Us

Welcome to Shiny Teeth!

At Shiny Teeth: Your Local Smile Studio, we are committed to making dental care a positive and enjoyable experience for everyone. Nestled in the heart of Maplewood, our charming, family-owned practice is dedicated to promoting healthy smiles in a warm, welcoming environment.

Our team of experienced professionals offers a wide range of services, from routine cleanings to advanced cosmetic treatments. We believe that every smile tells a story, and we’re here to help you tell yours! With our innovative “Smile Club” membership, we make it easy and affordable for families to prioritize their dental health.

But we’re more than just a dental office. We host community events like “Family Fun Days,” where children can learn about oral hygiene through fun activities. Our cozy waiting area, complete with a coffee corner and a play area, ensures that everyone feels at home from the moment they step inside.

At Shiny Teeth, we not only care for your dental health but also for our planet, using eco-friendly products and practices. We invite you to visit us and discover the joy of a healthy, beautiful smile!
