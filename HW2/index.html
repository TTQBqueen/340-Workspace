Brightening Smiles, One Family at a Time

Shiny Teeth: Your Local Smile Studio

At Shiny Teeth, we believe that everyone deserves a bright, healthy smile. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to providing quality dental care in a warm, welcoming environment. Whether you’re due for a routine cleaning or looking to enhance your smile with cosmetic treatments, we’re here to help. Visit us today and discover the joy of a healthy smile!

Nestled in the heart of Maplewood, Shiny Teeth is not your average dental office. This charming, family-owned business is dedicated to making oral care fun and accessible for everyone. From the moment you step inside, you’re greeted by bright colors, cheerful décor, and a friendly team that feels more like family than staff.

At Shiny Teeth, the mission is simple: to promote healthy smiles in a welcoming environment. They offer a range of services, from routine cleanings to innovative cosmetic treatments, all tailored to meet the needs of their diverse clientele. The standout feature? Their “Smile Club” membership, which provides affordable care plans designed for families and individuals, making it easier to prioritize dental health without breaking the bank.

But it’s not just about dental services. Shiny Teeth hosts community events like “Family Fun Days,” where kids can learn about oral hygiene through interactive games and activities. The shop also features a cozy waiting area with a coffee corner, books for all ages, and a play area to keep little ones entertained.

With a commitment to sustainability, Shiny Teeth uses eco-friendly products and practices, ensuring that every smile not only shines but also contributes to a healthier planet. Whether you’re coming in for a routine check-up or a whitening treatment, you’ll leave with a brighter smile and a lighter heart.

At Shiny Teeth, they believe every smile tells a story. Come in and let them help you tell yours!
